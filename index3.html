Firstly in my opinion, that would depend. Some graffiti are art, some aren't. For example, if it's drawn in a ridiculous place and it's disturbing, it's not art and another example, Karanfil street has a lot of graffiti and I think they look very nice, and it's an art. moreover, from my point of view, graffiti in general is done in the wrong places. For example, pictures in government institutions, especially lonely and bad ones are vandalism. If it is placed on a building for both image and content without the owner's permission. it is vandalism. Moreover, think about it: if you have a house or apartment and someone sees it as a blank canvas to create art, would you like it if you didn't approve? Finally, those who only write their names, gang signs or spray paint on street/road signs is pure vandalism.
